    • About Borg El Arab Technological University (BATU) Borg El Arab Technological University (BATU) is one of Egypt’s pioneering technological universities, established to bridge the gap between academic education and industry needs. Located in Borg El Arab, Alexandria, BATU offers advanced technological education designed to prepare students for the modern job market. Key Features of BATU: Industry-Oriented Education: The university follows a practical, hands-on approach to learning, ensuring students gain real-world experience. German-Model Education System: BATU adopts an educational framework similar to German technological universities, emphasizing applied sciences and vocational training. State-of-the-Art Facilities: The campus includes modern laboratories, workshops, and research centers to enhance student learning. Collaboration with Industries: BATU works closely with companies and industries to provide students with internships and job opportunities. With a focus on innovation, technology, and practical training, BATU aims to produce highly skilled graduates who can contribute effectively to Egypt’s technological and industrial growth.
